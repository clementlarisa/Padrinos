Started in 1972, Padrino's now has core businesses of retail & wholesale of musical instruments. Located at many outlets all over Sicily, Padrino's is the best place to purchase music accessories and related items.
Our skilled staff will undoubtedly offer you support while choosing your product of interest. Patience and manners are what defines us.

Come check that electric guitar you've always wanted. We most definitely have it somewhere near you! Or you could just swing by for a cup of coffee. We have that, too.

Our store in Palermo has been voted as a Flagship European Store with over 10,000 musical instruments on display.
